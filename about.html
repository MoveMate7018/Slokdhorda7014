About MoveMate

The driving force behind India's most reliable logistics

Our Story

Founded in 2020, MoveMate began with a simple mission: to make goods transportation simple, transparent, and reliable for everyone. We saw a fragmented industry plagued by delays, hidden fees, and uncertainty. We knew there had to be a better way.

Starting with just three trucks and a dedicated team, we embraced technology to build a platform that connects customers with verified drivers instantly. Today, we are a leading logistics partner with a network spanning 1200+ cities, powered by a commitment to service, safety, and customer satisfaction.
